  Generally speaking, I am against using any kind of generative AI. Based off of previous research from other classes as well as my vast knowledge on the subject, generative AI poses no benefit. Generative AI is built off of other artists work, using work that was stolen and used without concent of the artist to build the program. If you see an AI generated piece, it likely used the hard work of artists to make the program to create something with no emotion, no personality behind it. On top of the ethical problems of the stolen work, AI is also very harmful to the environment. Ai generated content makes for emotionless pieces, and is creating a generation of lazy individuals who would rather make something in seconds rather than put their time into something.
